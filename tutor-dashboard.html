PSAT 8/9 R&W — Tutor Dashboard

Every student's sessions, pulled from your published Google Sheet. Accuracy beside retention, because accuracy on its own always flatters.

How do I get these URLs?
In your Google Sheet: File → Share → Publish to web → pick the Homework tab → format Comma-separated values (.csv) → Publish → copy the link. Repeat for the Sessions tab and paste each on its own line. Google serves published CSVs with CORS, so nothing on the Apps Script side needs changing.

Both tabs matter and they carry different halves of the picture: retention is only ever posted by homework, while tab-switches and the practice breakdown only come from sessions. With one tab you get a real but partial read.

Columns are matched by header name against what the backend writes — Logged at, Student, Type, Day / Focus / Skills, Score, Total, Seconds, Raw payload — and anything without a column of its own is read out of Raw payload, which is where retention has quietly been all along.
